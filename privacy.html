Simple Tides Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-03

Simple Tides is designed to provide tide, weather, and marine information. This page explains what data is requested and how it is used.

Location Data

If you allow location access, your browser location is used only to center the map, estimate station distance and route timing, and improve local station discovery. Location is not required to use the app.

Third-Party Data Sources

Simple Tides requests data from NOAA CO-OPS, weather.gov, Open-Meteo, Sunrise-Sunset, and FarmSense to provide forecasts and chart details.

Personal Data

No account is required. Personal data is not sold. The app does not store personal profiles.

Analytics

Site analytics may be used to understand aggregate traffic and improve site performance.
