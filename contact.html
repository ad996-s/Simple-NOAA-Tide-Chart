Contact

Questions, bug reports, bad station data, or general feedback — here's how to reach us.

Email

The fastest way to reach the team is s.tides99@gmail.com.

Reporting a data issue? Please include the station name (or NOAA station ID if you have it), what you saw, and roughly when you saw it. That's usually enough for us to track down the source.

What we can help with

  • Bugs or broken features on the map or station detail view
  • A station showing missing or clearly incorrect data
  • Feedback on the Smart Bite Score or solunar activity display
  • General questions about how Simple Tides works

Response time

Simple Tides is an independent project, so replies may take a few days. We read every message.

You might also find your answer faster in the FAQ or Privacy Policy.
