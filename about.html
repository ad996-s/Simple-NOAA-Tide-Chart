About Simple Tides

Simple Tides is a map-first marine planning app focused on quick station discovery and practical on-water timing insights.

Core Features

  • Nearby NOAA station discovery on an interactive map
  • Upcoming high and low tide timeline
  • Tide chart preview for selected stations
  • Weather, marine, and solunar context
  • Location-aware route timing and map centering

Data Sources

Simple Tides aggregates publicly available data from NOAA CO-OPS, weather.gov, Open-Meteo, Sunrise-Sunset, and FarmSense.
